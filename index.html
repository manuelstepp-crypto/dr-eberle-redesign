Company

Five Generations of
Clever Chemistry

Dr. Eberle Clever Chemistry GmbH develops and manufactures chemicals for leather processing in all applications for the automotive, footwear, bag, and upholstery industries and supplies these via a worldwide distribution network.

The family-owned company, now in its fifth generation, was founded in 1875 by Gustav Eberle, a saddler dissatisfied with leather quality, who encouraged his son to study chemistry. Dr. Reinhard Eberle assumed leadership in 1984, expanding product offerings and Asian market development. His son, Dr. Florian Eberle, a biochemist, joined the company in 2014.

Our philosophy is clear: Corporate responsibility is required for the success of the company — balancing economic success, environmental responsibility, and social appropriateness.

Products

Leather & Textile Auxiliaries

We develop and manufacture the full range of chemicals for leather and textile processing — from the raw hide to the finished product.

Leather Auxiliaries

Beamhouse

Soaking agents, liming, deliming, bating, degreasing and pickle products including Eborol K, Ebrozid KW, Neopalin T 106, Purgazym WS.

Fatliquors

Basic, cationic, hydrophobic, lecithin, main, polymer, and synthetic variants. Featuring Licrol and Ebenin series.

Biopolymers

Innovative bio-based solutions including Ebrolin PFE and Ebotan PCK for sustainable leather processing.

Dyeing Auxiliaries

Specialized products for leather dyeing: Ebrofix D, Ebrolin 66, and Ebrolin 98.

Retanning Agents

Ebotan series including AC, DA 50 FF, GA, and RA 40 for optimal retanning results.

Syntans

Eleven Ebotan variants for versatile leather treatment and finishing applications.

Special Products

Specialized solutions including Ebosoft LT and Neopalin AS for specific processing needs.

Indian Syntans

Specialized regional syntan offerings tailored for the Indian leather market.

Textile Auxiliaries

Pretreatment Agents

Eborol BW, Eborol spontan, Neopalin DOR, and Neopalin NWD for textile preparation.

Dyeing and Printing

Eboclean VA 54, Ebotex U, Ebotex W conc., and Vivazon VH conc. for textile coloring.

Chemical Finishing

Ebosoft S-GT and Ebosoft S-HG for professional textile finishing applications.

Antifoaming Agents

Mikrokoll DOS, Mikrokoll XN, and Mikrokoll XS for effective foam control.

Innovation

Innovative Concepts for High-Quality Products

The development of tailor-made solutions for the specific market requirements of our customers is the key of our research and development strategy.

Our product development center in Germany brings together chemists and leather technicians who collaborate to create cutting-edge solutions for all stages of leather processing.

International teamwork and an open hierarchy allow us to identify the perfect chemistry for any leather article. We continuously develop innovative products meeting industry demands, producing special products of constant and high quality.

We participate in international leather trade fairs to engage customers, discuss market trends, and showcase our latest developments.
